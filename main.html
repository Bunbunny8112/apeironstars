APEIRON SACRED STARS

In Apeiron, there are 11, 498 stars, each of which belongs to a unique constellation.
Stars are similar to the bases of other games, wherein each star has one or more orbital tracks, which will give an aging boost to your planets if you "park" them on the empty orbital tracks. Aging your planet quicker will allow you to access relic slots, which you may use to place relics on your planet, enabling you to power up EDEN and your avatar.
